Participate in the Water Futures Market

The Exchange aggregates all of the data in the database to arive at an aggregated water value for the State of Colorado using a proprietary formula.  This is updated with the most recent receipt of water transactions.  Use the MyCWETokens to purchase and trade contracts on the Exchange's Water Futures Marketplace (tranaction fees apply).
